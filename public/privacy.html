Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026  ·  Last updated: March 2026
01

Overview

IronMap ("we", "our", or "the app") is a gym training and daily habit tracking app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

The short version: IronMap stores your gym data locally on your device. We do not require an account. We do not sell your data. We do not run ads. The app works without an internet connection for all core features.
02

03

Community Features

If you join a community using a community code, the following data is synced to our backend (Supabase) to enable the community feed:

  • Your anonymous device ID
  • Your chosen display name
  • Your daily check-in completion data (items completed, streak count)
  • Optional short posts you choose to share with the community

This data is associated with your device ID only — not your name, email, or any other identity. Other community members can see your display name, streak, and check-in completion status.

Community data is voluntary. You can use all workout tracking and personal check-in features without joining any community. Leaving a community removes your data from the community feed.
04

Apple HealthKit (iOS)

On iOS with Apple Watch, IronMap requests read access to heart rate data during workouts. This data is:

  • Displayed live on your Apple Watch during active sessions
  • Saved to Apple Health as a workout record when your session ends
  • Never transmitted to IronMap's servers
  • Never used for advertising or analytics

IronMap does not write data to HealthKit other than workout records you explicitly complete. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → IronMap.

05

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

The only third-party service we use is:

  • Supabase — backend database for community features. Community check-in data (device ID, display name, completion status) is stored on Supabase servers. Supabase's privacy policy applies to data stored there.

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or third-party tracking in the app.

06

Data Retention & Deletion

Local data (workout logs, check-ins, settings) is stored on your device and remains until you delete the app or use the "Delete all data" option in Settings.

Community data on our servers is retained while you are a member of a community. When you leave a community, your check-in data for that community is removed from the feed. You can request full deletion of all server-side data associated with your device ID by contacting us.

07

Children's Privacy

IronMap is designed for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through IronMap, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as the app's features evolve. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of IronMap after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
